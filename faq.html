FAQS

Looking for more information about our pastries? You can find your answers here!

DO YOU OFFER GLUTEN FREE ITEMS?

Yes, we do offer gluten-free products in the shop but we can accommodate to anyone who wants gluten-free products.

DO YOU MAKE ANY VEGAN ITEMS?

We currently do have vegan options in the shop. In order to get them, we need to specially make them prior to your arrival.

HOW DO I KEEP MY PURCHASES FRESH?

All pastries are made fresh in the morning and throughout the day. Towards the end of the day we give most of our products away to keep them from going to waste.

DO YOU HAVE DELIVERY SERVICES?

We do have a catering option that we can use as a delivery option on orders bigger than 10 pastries.
